Epic Ride Weather — Your Go-To App
You're already using the best tool for this. Epic Ride Weather gives you hour-by-hour conditions along your actual route — wind direction, rain, temperature and more. Use it from here for each ride.
🌐 Open Web App 📱 iOS App 🤖 Android App
Quick Launch — Check Weather for a Saved Route
Select a route and your ride date — opens Epic Ride Weather ready to go.
Tip: In Epic Ride Weather, use "Import from Strava" to load your saved Strava routes directly. This gives you the most accurate along-route forecast.
How to Get the Best from Epic Ride Weather + Strava
1
Plan route on Strava
Create or find your route in Strava Route Builder. Save it to your library.
2
Open Epic Ride Weather
Tap "Add Ride" → "Import from Strava" → pick your route.
3
Set date & start time
ERW shows conditions hour-by-hour along the exact route — wind, rain, temp.
4
Share the forecast
Screenshot the ERW forecast and send it to members via the Comms tab.
